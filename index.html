About Me

Hi! My name is Septa Shasy Ramadani, a passionate Creative Designer from Central Java, Indonesia. I’m currently pursuing my studies in Informatics at STIMIK Tunas Bangsa Banjarnegara, where I explore the intersection of creativity and technology. With a strong interest in UI/UX design, web development, and visual storytelling, I enjoy transforming ideas into engaging digital experiences. My journey in design started from curiosity, and has now grown into a commitment to create meaningful, user-friendly, and aesthetically pleasing works.
I love experimenting with new tools, exploring design trends, and continuously learning to improve my skills. Whether it’s designing interfaces, working with typography, or crafting layouts, I strive to combine functionality with creativity. Beyond design, I value collaboration, problem-solving, and the process of turning concepts into reality. My goal is to build not only visually appealing projects but also experiences that leave a lasting impact.
